1- What is cross-chain currency exchange?
Cross-chain exchange allows users to swap cryptocurrencies between different blockchain networks without the need for a third party. For example, you can convert USDT from the Ethereum network to the TRX network.

2- How do I verify the reliability of exchange providers on your site?
We evaluate providers using strict criteria that include security, speed, reasonable fees, and reviews from previous users. We ensure that only the most reliable and transparent providers in the market are featured on our site.

3- Can I track the status of the exchange?
Yes, most of the platforms we recommend offer tools to track the exchange status in real-time using the order ID.

4- Does pusdt.io charge any fees from users?
No, we do not charge any fees from users. Our platform is completely free to use. The fees we receive come from our partnership program with the exchange providers. This allows us to offer the service without passing any additional costs to the users.

5- Why can I trust you?
No registration and no need to share your personal details. We don't hold your funds; all exchanges take place instantly in fully automatic mode.
